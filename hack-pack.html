Challenges

Cultural

Refugees are incredibly vulnerable. Talking to these people takes time, patience and the upmost empathy. With refugees being a broad term, and coming from all around the globe we need a way of prioritising which languages are in the highest demand.

User Experience

The app needs to balance the feeling of the stories being public, with anonymity and security. As UX-ers we need to fully grasp the situation, overcoming the strong cultural differences between our two worlds. HerStory needs to be as accessible as possible, capturing every possible format.

Technology

Due to the accessibility demands HerStory will need to integrate with a lot of apps. We need to capture stories from WhatsApp, SnapChat, DropBox, and SMS just to name a few.

Safety and Security

Refugees, female refugees especially are in the most vulnerable state when we take into consideration the types of stories this platform aims to capture. Our aim is to provide inspiration, security, encouragement and support through a community. Both comments and stories will need to be moderated in some way.

HerStory needs your suggestions and expertise on how to solve these issues!
